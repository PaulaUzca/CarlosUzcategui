About

I am from Venezuela, I worked at the Universidad de Los Andes (ULA) in Mérida for about 30 years. I moved to Colombia in 2015, since then I work at Universidad Industrial de Santander (Bucaramanga). I did my undergraduate studies at ULA, my master degree at the Instituto Venezolano de Investigaciones Científica in Caracas and my PhD at California Institute of Technology in the USA.
